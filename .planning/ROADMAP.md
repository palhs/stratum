# Roadmap: Stratum

## Overview

Stratum is built in phases following the hard dependency chain of its two-pipeline architecture. v1.0 delivered infrastructure and data ingestion (Phases 1-2). v2.0 built the analytical reasoning engine (Phases 3-9). v3.0 will deliver the user-facing layer.

## Milestones

- ✅ **v1.0 Infrastructure and Data Ingestion** — Phases 1-2 (shipped 2026-03-09)
- ✅ **v2.0 Analytical Reasoning Engine** — Phases 3-9 (shipped 2026-03-17)
